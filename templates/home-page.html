NetraPath is an innovative assistive tool developed at the SRM Techno Hackathon 2025. It empowers individuals with visual impairments to navigate their surroundings with confidence by leveraging cutting-edge technology.

Our mission is to create a more inclusive world by providing real-time assistance through text recognition, object detection, and audio feedback.
